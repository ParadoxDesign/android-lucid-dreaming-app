Accelerometer Calibration- Science!

Please lay the device flat on your bed, plug in the power and select "start calibration" from the menu

The device will calibrate itself relative to that position. Recommended spot is 16"/40 cm from the center of your pillow. If your bed is very springy, you can put the phone further away. If it is stiff, put it closer to your pillow. Select the Start calibration option from the menu to start

Calibration progress is displayed in the status bar

Check your phone for battery recall, charger recall, etc, if you are concerned about fire safety!

Phone calibration is extremely sensitive to movement, please avoid touching the phone or the bed during calibration
