Working with sleep cycles

With 3 days (up to 7) of data you can plot them in history and observe your Sleep Stages. Look for elevated activity in 90-110 minute intervals. Lucid dreaming app comes with a pre-configured SmartTimer events that you can adjust slightly to fit your sleep stage events.
  • Press the "change config" button below. Select "SmartTimerConfig.txt.gzip".
  • Configure Smart timer events
  • Activate events when you want the reminder to play
  • Assign a combination of voice, vibrate or light reminders to each event
  • For on-movement reminders, configure the movement intensity in Advanced options : REM Prediction preferences : REM Prediction Activity Threshold
  Important: While on the history screen,the lower history graph shows reminders based on the current Smart Timer config. Use this feature to adjust the configuration file to set up REM events 5 minutes before your reported dreams Examine the list of additional features that you may take advantage of.
