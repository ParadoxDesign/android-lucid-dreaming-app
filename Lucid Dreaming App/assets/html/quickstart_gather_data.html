Gather sleep cycle data.

For at least 3 days run the app without reminders and collect sleep data. You will need these to figure out your sleep cycles and REM sleep episodes.
  • Disable all reminders: preferences> Play voice reminders
  • Start the app when you go to sleep.
  • Upon waking from a dream, draw a "dream" gesture on the screen (click show gestures to see how it looks)
  • In the morning, shut off the app using menu>exit on the clock screen
  • Verify the graph was saved. App home screen>view data>load graph
With 3 days of data, you are ready to proceed with history analysis.
