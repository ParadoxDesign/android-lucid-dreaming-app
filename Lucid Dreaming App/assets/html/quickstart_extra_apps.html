Additional Apps

The app comes with no built-in reminders to keep the download size small. Here are your options for audio reminders:
  • Get a sound recorder software for free from Android Market or find a suitable mp3/Wav reminder.
  • Create a folder on the SDCard named "Recordings".
  • Put the MP3 in the sdcard/Recordings folder, rename the MP3 file into "lucid.mp3". You now have a default voice reminder.
  • If no SDCard/Recordings/lucid.mp3 is found, the app will play default alarm (may be annoying!)
  • Read more on how to configure voice reminders
Please note: the app uses your device's built in media player to play audio.
