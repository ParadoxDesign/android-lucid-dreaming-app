Calibrating the app

There are over 400 Android devices out there, so the app has to be calibrated for your device. Upon starting the app for the first time, it is not calibrated and a calibration screen is displayed.
  • Click "Quick calibration" button - this will calibrate the app for most devices
  • Look for the yellow bar filling up, when calibration is complete, the app shows clock screeen.
  • If the yellow bar on top does not fill up (Droid, Samsung phones, etc). Pick up the phone in your hand, gently roll it around, filling up the bar.
  • Calibration is complete when the calibration activity closes and clock screen is visible
